# Stakeholder Mapping

## Project Title

**AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation using Salesforce Agentforce**

## Overview

Stakeholder mapping identifies the individuals and teams involved in the project, their responsibilities, level of influence, and communication requirements. Clearly defining stakeholder roles helps ensure effective collaboration, timely decision-making, and successful project delivery.

---

# Stakeholder Identification

| Stakeholder                   | Role                   | Responsibility                                                                                     | Influence |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------- |
| Business Manager              | Project Sponsor        | Defines business goals, approves project scope, and evaluates project success.                     | High      |
| Project Manager               | Project Management     | Plans the project, monitors progress, manages timelines, and coordinates the team.                 | High      |
| Salesforce Administrator      | Platform Administrator | Configures Salesforce, manages users, security, and system maintenance.                            | High      |
| Salesforce Developer          | Developer              | Develops custom objects, Flows, Prompt Builder templates, Agentforce actions, and Lightning pages. | High      |
| AI Administrator              | AI Configuration       | Configures Prompt Builder, Agentforce, and AI-enabled features for customer support.               | Medium    |
| Customer Support Manager      | Business User          | Defines customer service workflows and validates business requirements.                            | High      |
| Customer Support Agent        | End User               | Creates, updates, and resolves customer support cases using the system.                            | Medium    |
| Quality Assurance (QA) Tester | Testing                | Performs functional testing, validates AI responses, and reports defects.                          | Medium    |
| System Administrator          | Infrastructure         | Maintains system availability, backups, and security configurations.                               | Medium    |
| Customers                     | End Beneficiaries      | Submit support requests and provide feedback on service quality.                                   | Low       |

---

# Stakeholder Responsibilities

### Business Manager

* Define project objectives.
* Approve project scope and deliverables.
* Review business outcomes.

### Project Manager

* Coordinate project activities.
* Track milestones and timelines.
* Manage stakeholder communication.

### Salesforce Administrator

* Configure Salesforce environment.
* Manage users and permissions.
* Maintain system security.

### Salesforce Developer

* Develop automation using Salesforce Flow.
* Configure Prompt Builder and Agentforce.
* Customize Lightning pages and business logic.

### AI Administrator

* Configure AI prompts.
* Monitor AI-generated responses.
* Optimize AI performance.

### Customer Support Manager

* Validate customer service workflows.
* Review AI recommendations.
* Monitor support operations.

### Customer Support Agent

* Handle customer cases.
* Use AI-generated summaries and recommendations.
* Update case records.

### QA Tester

* Test application functionality.
* Validate automation and AI outputs.
* Report and verify issue fixes.

### System Administrator

* Monitor system performance.
* Manage backups and platform reliability.
* Ensure platform availability.

### Customers

* Raise service requests.
* Track support status.
* Provide feedback to improve service quality.

# Communication Strategy

The project follows a structured communication approach:

* Weekly meetings are conducted with business stakeholders to review project progress.
* Development updates are shared during sprint review meetings.
* QA reports are generated after each testing cycle.
* Customer support managers validate workflows before deployment.
* User feedback is collected after implementation to support continuous improvement.

---

# Conclusion

Effective stakeholder management is essential for the successful implementation of the AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation system. Clearly defined roles, regular communication, and active collaboration ensure that business requirements are met, risks are minimized, and the solution delivers value to both the organization and its customers.
