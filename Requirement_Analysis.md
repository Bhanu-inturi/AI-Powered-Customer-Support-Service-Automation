# Requirement Analysis

# AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation

## Project Overview

The AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation system is designed to streamline customer service operations for an international electronics and gadgets company. The organization receives a high volume of customer inquiries related to product issues, warranty claims, order tracking, returns, and general support. Handling these requests manually often results in delayed responses, inconsistent service quality, and increased workload for support agents.

This project leverages Salesforce Service Cloud, Salesforce Flow, Prompt Builder, and Agentforce to automate customer support processes and improve operational efficiency. AI-powered assistance helps agents respond faster while maintaining accurate and consistent customer communication.

---

# Problem Statement

The existing customer support process is largely manual, leading to:

* Slow response times
* High workload for support agents
* Inconsistent customer service quality
* Difficulty tracking support cases
* Delays in warranty claim processing
* Limited visibility into customer support performance

An intelligent Salesforce-based solution is required to automate repetitive tasks and improve customer experience.

---

# Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

* Automate customer support processes using Salesforce.
* Improve response time through AI-powered assistance.
* Generate automated case summaries using Prompt Builder.
* Simplify case routing using Salesforce Flows.
* Provide agents with intelligent response suggestions.
* Improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
* Ensure secure handling of customer information.

---

# Business Requirements

The system should allow users to:

* Manage customer information.
* Track products and orders.
* Create and manage support cases.
* Process warranty claims.
* Route cases automatically to the correct support team.
* Generate AI-powered case summaries.
* Provide AI-assisted customer responses.
* Track case status until resolution.
* Generate reports and dashboards for management.

---

# Functional Requirements

The application should provide the following features:
